Joe Peters

is an American functional glass artist who specializes in the sculpture of natural life-forms. An avid scuba diver, Joe has been heavily influenced by wildlife and the natural world. Recently, he has relocated to Everdream Studio with other flameworkers, N8, Adam G, WJC, Elbo, and Eusheen. On his own and through collaborative engagements, Joe continues to create pieces for glass enthusiasts worldwide.
